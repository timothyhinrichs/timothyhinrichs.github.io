Recently we've seen a growing number of cloud management products that support policy languages, a trend that will eventually enable a purely policy-based approach to cloud management.

RuleYourCloud is a blog dedicated to furthering the trend toward policy-based cloud management--toward a world in which we rule the cloud with rules.
